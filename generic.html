Find Us

To find us, take I-4 to Downtown Orlando and exit at Princeton Street, Exit 85. Go west on Princeton.Princeton becomes Smith Street westbound. Follow Smith through the stoplight at Edgewater. We are the second building on the right. The building is raspberry-plum pink trimmed with chocolate and a chocolate awning with Truffles and Trifles on the awning. There is plenty of parking behind the building.
